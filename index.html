Why Bangkok?

Bangkok, the city of angels

Bangkok is officially referred to as Krung Thep; its literal meaning in the language is "City of Angels." The full ceremonial name of this city is recognised as one of the longest place names in the world, comprising 169 characters. The term "angels" is a word that represents guardian deities believed to guard the city and its people. Such an attractive title comprehends Bangkok's attractions, best captured in its lively atmosphere, markets, amazing temples, and hospitality, a favourite destination worthy of its heavenly name.

Bangkok is a city that brings together ancient culture with modernity. Large roads are full of excitement, lined with vibrant markets, ornate temples, and breathtaking street food that will leave the hearts of all travellers in the world. The history, architecture, lively nightlife and shopping bazaar continue to thrive within it. It can be a visit to the Grand Grand Palace or, more likely, a boat trip along the Chao Phraya River. Bangkok promises unforgettable adventures that are sure to leave you enchanted.
